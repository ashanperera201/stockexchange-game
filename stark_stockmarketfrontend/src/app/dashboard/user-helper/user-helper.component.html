 Steps to follow

Step 01 : Use your google account to login to the game.

Step 02 : Click on "Create group" menu on dash board.Give relavent data and create a group.

Step 03 : Click on "Join group" menu on dash board.

Step 04 : Select a group and click on "Join me" button to join to the game.

Step 05 : Click start button on "Lobby" (available only for the group creator).

Step 06 : Select a company from dropdown menu.

Step 07 : Select Buy/Sell option from the dropdown menue.

Step 08 : Enter the shares quantity.

Step 09 : Click on "Process" button.

Step 10 : Repeat STEP 06 to STEP 09 for each round.

NOTE:

-You must wait at lobby until other players join to the game.(Minimum: 2 players,Maximum:4 players or more)

-Each game consist of 5 rounds.

-In each round you can buy/sell any number of shares within 2 minute.
